About This Project

Struggling to find the best time to hit the gym without the crowd? Our tool helps ActiveSG users make informed decisions by visualizing gym capacity trends, whether you're avoiding peak hours or just curious about crowd patterns. 🚀

How It Works

We web scrape ActiveSG gym capacity data from their Live Tracking Website, eeping our database up-to-date with the latest trends. The data is then transformed into clear visualizations reflecting:

✅ Crowd Level Fluctuations

✅ Weekday vs. Weekend Trends

What’s Next?

We’re constantly improving! Future features include:

📊 Holiday Adjustments – Factoring in Public/School Holidays

📊 Forecasting Model – ML-powered Capacity Predictions
